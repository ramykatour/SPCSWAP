            <h2>Introduction</h2>
            <p>SPC SWAP is a fork of PancakeSwap that allows users to perform token swaps and provide liquidity on the SPC blockchain network (SPC Chain).</p>
        </section>

        <section id="swap">
            <h2>How to Use the Swap Feature</h2>
            <p>Follow these steps to swap tokens on SPC SWAP:</p>
            <ol>
                <li>Visit the SPC SWAP website.</li>
                <li>Connect your wallet to the SPC Chain (as described in the "Connect to SPC Chain" section above).</li>
                <li>In the "From" field, select the token you want to swap.</li>
                <li>In the "To" field, select the token you want to receive.</li>
                <li>Enter the amount of the "From" token you want to swap.</li>
                <li>Click the "Swap" button to execute the transaction.</li>
                <li>Confirm the transaction in your wallet to complete the swap.</li>
            </ol>
        </section>

        <section id="liquidity">
            <h2>How to Provide Liquidity</h2>
            <p>Follow these steps to add liquidity to SPC SWAP:</p>
            <ol>
                <li>Navigate to the "Add Liquidity" section on the SPC SWAP website.</li>
                <li>Choose the two tokens you want to provide liquidity for.</li>
                <li>Enter the amount of each token you want to add to the liquidity pool.</li>
                <li>Click the "Add" button to submit the liquidity transaction.</li>
                <li>Confirm the transaction in your wallet to complete the liquidity addition.</li>
            </ol>
        </section>

        <section id="fees">
            <h2>Transaction Fees</h2>
            <p>There is a small fee applied to each swap and liquidity addition. These fees are used to reward liquidity providers and maintain the stability of the platform.</p>
        </section>
